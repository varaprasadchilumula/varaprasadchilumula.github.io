Purpose site

We provide detailed Weather Forecasts over a 12 day period updated four times a day. Our interactive weather maps can be customized to show forecasts of temperature, weather and wind. You can also overlay pressure and live weather observations on maps of any country. Select our weather today forecast pages to get 3 hourly detail and live weather reports. Browse our weather photo albums showing a great selection of weather extremes from around the world including hurricanes, floods, storms, tornadoes and major snow events.
